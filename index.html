About Us

PROTECTING EVERY LIFE

RAKSHA-FORCE is India's next-generation emergency response network, reducing the gap between crisis and help. We coordinate across police, fire, medical and disaster management services.

Founded in 2022, operating across 28 states, we've supported over 48,000 emergency responses through technology-driven coordination and community-powered networks.
